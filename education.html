Education

DRI's qualified education team members know the effort it takes to plan and run a successful education program for a group - because we've all done it during our teaching careers! We make it easy for you; you can be sure that your group will come away amazed, excited and enthused - no matter what their age.

Their programs about Victoria's living marine treasures are run where you want them. They have both incursions and excursions. Talk to their team to find the best ones for you. And by supporting the DRI through the education programs, you are also, in an active and tangible way, supporting all living marine treasures for the future. Thank you.
